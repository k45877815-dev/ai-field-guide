No. 002 — The catalog

The directory

Eighteen specimens across six families. Jump straight to a family, or scroll through the whole catalog.

Writing & content Coding & dev Image & design Video & audio Research & productivity Chatbots & assistants

Family I

Writing & content

Drafting, rewriting, and polishing text — long-form, ad copy, or everyday email.

No. 001

Grammarly

Grammar & tone editor

A writing assistant that checks grammar, clarity, and tone as you type, built as a browser extension and desktop app that layers onto whatever you're already writing in.

grammarly.com

No. 002

Jasper

Marketing copy generator

Built for marketing teams producing ad copy, blog drafts, and brand-voice content at volume, with templates aimed at repeatable campaign work.

jasper.ai

No. 003

Copy.ai

Short-form copy & workflows

Generates short-form marketing copy and increasingly focuses on multi-step content workflows rather than one-off text generation.

copy.ai

Family II

Coding & dev

Autocomplete, chat-based debugging, and agentic assistants that work inside your editor or terminal.

No. 004

GitHub Copilot

In-editor pair programmer

Suggests code completions and full functions directly inside popular editors, and can answer questions about the surrounding codebase.

github.com/features/copilot

No. 005

Cursor

AI-native code editor

A full code editor built around AI assistance from the ground up, with multi-file edits and chat grounded in your actual project.

cursor.com

No. 006

Replit Agent

Build-and-deploy agent

Goes further than autocomplete by scaffolding, running, and deploying a working app from a plain-language description inside the browser.

replit.com

Family III

Image & design

Text-to-image models and design copilots for mockups, illustration, and brand assets.

No. 007

Midjourney

Stylized image generation

Known for a distinctive, painterly default aesthetic, generated through short text prompts inside Discord or its own web app.

midjourney.com

No. 008

Adobe Firefly

Commercially-licensed generation

Image and effects generation trained on licensed and public-domain material, built to slot into Photoshop and other Adobe apps.

firefly.adobe.com

No. 009

Canva Magic Studio

Design copilot suite

A set of AI features layered into Canva's existing editor — background removal, layout suggestions, and text-to-image inside templates you can still hand-edit.

canva.com/magic-studio

Family IV

Video & audio

Generative video, voice cloning, and editing tools that cut hours out of production.

No. 010

Runway

Generative video editing

Text-to-video and video-editing tools aimed at filmmakers and motion designers, including scene extension and object removal.

runwayml.com

No. 011

ElevenLabs

Voice generation & cloning

Text-to-speech with a large library of voices, plus tools to clone a specific voice for narration, dubbing, or characters.

elevenlabs.io

No. 012

Descript

Edit video by editing text

Transcribes a recording and lets you cut the video by deleting words in the transcript, aimed at podcasters and video editors.

descript.com

Family V

Research & productivity

Search, summarization, and knowledge-base tools that sit between you and information overload.

No. 013

Perplexity

Answer engine

Search that returns a synthesized answer with cited sources rather than a page of links, aimed at research and fact-finding.

perplexity.ai

No. 014

Notion AI

In-workspace assistant

Summarizing, drafting, and Q&A built directly into Notion's docs and databases, so it works with content you've already organized there.

notion.com/product/ai

No. 015

Otter.ai

Meeting transcription

Joins calls to transcribe and summarize meetings in real time, with searchable notes and action items afterward.

otter.ai

Family VI

Chatbots & assistants

The general-purpose conversational models most other tools in this guide are built on top of.

No. 016

ChatGPT

General-purpose assistant

OpenAI's conversational assistant, used for everything from drafting and coding help to research and brainstorming, with a large plugin and custom-GPT ecosystem.

chatgpt.com

No. 017

Claude

General-purpose assistant

Anthropic's conversational assistant, available via chat, API, and desktop apps, with particular strength in long-document work and coding.

claude.ai

No. 018

Gemini

General-purpose assistant

Google's conversational assistant, integrated across Search, Workspace, and Android, with native multimodal input across text, image, and audio.

gemini.google.com
