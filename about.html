No. 003 — About

A catalog, kept by hand.

AI Field Guide is an independently maintained directory of AI tools, organized by what they're actually for rather than by hype. Every entry is added because it does something genuinely useful in its category — not because it paid to be listed.

New tools ship constantly, and most directories either drown you in every product that's ever launched, or go stale within a month. This one is small on purpose: eighteen specimens, six families, reviewed and pruned rather than endlessly expanded.

How entries get added

A tool earns a place in the directory if it's genuinely useful in its category, still actively maintained, and can be tried without a mandatory sales call. Descriptions are written independently, in plain language, based on what the tool does — not copied from its marketing site.

Suggest a tool

Missing something that belongs here? Open an issue on the GitHub repository with the tool name and what it's best at.
