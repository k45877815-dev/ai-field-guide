Why this exists

Most directories are noise. This one is a catalog.

Anyone can scrape a list of AI tools. The useful part is the taxonomy: knowing which family a tool belongs to, what it's actually built for, and which one to reach for first. That's what gets rebuilt here every time a new specimen is added.

Who it's for

Freelancers, small teams & the curious

If you don't have time to test forty tools before picking one, this guide exists to shortcut that. Every entry links straight to the tool — no affiliate walls, no forced signups.
